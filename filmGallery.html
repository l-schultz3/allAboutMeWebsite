Film is a photographic medium that has existed since the 1800's. Photography film is flexible plastic that is coated in chemicals that, when exposed to light physically change.

Over the past year and a half, I have been working with film on various cameras, the oldest being from 1976. There are many reasons that I like using film, but the main one would be the uncertainty you have while using it. Each picture that you take is a gamble, costing both money and time.
